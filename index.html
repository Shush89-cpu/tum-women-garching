Our Mission

We advocate for gender equality and empower women students at TUM's Garching campus through support, awareness and community.

Initiatives

  • Mentorship programs connecting new students with experienced mentors.
  • Workshops on leadership, negotiation and personal development.
  • Advocacy for inclusive policies within departments and faculties.
